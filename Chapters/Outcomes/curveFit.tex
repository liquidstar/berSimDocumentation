\section{Curve Fitting}



The equation for AWGN Curve fitting is:
\begin{align*}
	\begin{split}
y=f(x) = 0.0005046x^9 &+ 0.02122x^8 -0.02507x^7 - 0.1269x^6 + 0.1545x^5\\ &+ 0.2027x^4 - 0.3122x^3 + 0.049 x^2 - 0.004642
\end{split}
\end{align*}
\begin{mathDef}
	\mathSymb{x}{SNR value in dB.}
	\mathSymb{y}{BER}
\end{mathDef}
